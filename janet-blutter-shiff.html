Janet Blutter Shiff, ASID

Janet Blutter Shiff, president of Blutter/Shiff Design Associates, has been involved with design of both residential and commercial interiors for more than 30 years.

Ms. Shiff received her B.A. from the University of Wisconsin, and completed her interior design education at Harrington Institute of Interior Design. To enhance her design skills and technique, she studied abroad, in Copenhagen, Denmark.

Ms. Shiff is a recognized professional in the design community, frequently asked to participate on industry discussion panels nationwide. She has appeared on the House & Garden cable network, and on the Discovery Channel. Her work has been published in trade magazines and consumer publications.

In addition to a vast number of residential clients throughout metropolitan Chicago, Ms. Shiff is currently working on projects in New York, Florida, Arizona, and California.
