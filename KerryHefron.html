Kerry Hefron, Office Administrator

Kerry Hefron is in her second year with Blutter Shiff. She holds a BA in Organizational Leadership and is currently pursuing an Interior Design degree. After spending more than a decade in Chicago's financial community, Kerry accepted a merchandising position with a prestigious retail organization helping transition her career towards interior design.

Kerry her excellent communication and organizational skills to her role as the day-to-day manager of the Blutter Shiff office while continuing to build upon her experience in the design field.
